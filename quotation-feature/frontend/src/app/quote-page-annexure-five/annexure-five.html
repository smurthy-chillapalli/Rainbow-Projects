Annexure V - Terms and Conditions of contract

  1. This contract shall be valid for a period of 12 months from the date of signing the contract. Any delay beyond the period of contract resulting due to either delay in shaft readiness, delay in releasing the payment as per the payment terms by the customer and changes in the price of the materials, the parties agree to enter into a revised contract incorporating the necessary price revision and also the fresh commitment dates for the supply and installation. In the event of customer refusing to execute the revised contract within 4 weeks of intimation of the preparation of the revised contract to the customer, the company shall be at liberty to treat the contract as cancelled by the customer and forfeit/claim the amount as mentioned in clause no 3 of this contract from the customer.

  2. The customer shall make the payment of the contract value in the following manner.

    • 50 % of the total contract value shall be paid by the customer to the company on the date of signing of the contract.
    • 40 % of the contract value shall be paid by the customer to the company against intimation of readiness of the material before shipment.
    • 10 % of the contract value shall be paid by the customer to the company against completion and handing over of the elevator. The customer shall pay the final payment on completion and handing over of the elevator and shall not be withheld for the final operating license. In the event of any delay in releasing the overdue payment the customer shall pay an interest at the rate of 18% pa upon the overdue amount from the date of amount falling due till the payment of entire outstanding to the company. The company reserves the right to dispose-off the equipment or discontinue the work at any time until payment is made as agreed.

  3. In the event of cancellation of contract by the customer, the company is entitled to forfeit the amount paid by the customer to the company or claim compensation in the following manner:

    • 25 % of the contract value if the order is cancelled before approval of Drawing.
    • 50 % of the contract value, if the contract is cancelled after the approval of general arrangement drawing.
    • 75 % of the contract value if the order is cancelled after dispatch of first consignment or with 10 days from the date of approval of general arrangement drawings but before delivery of materials.
    • 90% of the contract value if the order is cancelled after delivery of materials at site.
