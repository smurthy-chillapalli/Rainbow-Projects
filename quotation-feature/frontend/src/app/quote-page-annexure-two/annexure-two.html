Terms of Payment

The adjustment in price in this quotation may be billed by us as soon as the amount there of is ascertainable and shall be payable on demand. Payment shall be made pro rata per lift as follows:

  • 50% of the total contract value with your acceptance of this proposal.
  • 30% of the contract value against readiness of the material before shipment.
  • 15 % on completion of mechanical erection at site.
  • 5% against commissioning and handing over of the elevator.

The percentage of payment schedule need not indicative of actual work in progress. Any payment not made by the customer to the company at due date shall bear interest 18% per annum from such due date till the payment of such amount. We reserve the right to dispose off the equipment or to discontinue our work at any time until payment shall have been made as agreed and we have assurance satisfactory to us that subsequent payments will be made as they fall due, provided also, that this will in no way relieve you of your liabilities and obligations under this contract. The total days of delay in making payment as per the payment schedule from your end shall delay the supply, installation & Completion of Commissioning of the lift from our end proportionately
