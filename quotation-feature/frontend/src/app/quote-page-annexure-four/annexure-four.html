Annexure IV - Scope of Clients work – Site readiness

  • Scope of Clients work – Site readiness:
    1. The customer shall furnish within one week (or sooner if required) from the date of acceptance of this proposal, all required data for the performance of this contract to the company The company will furnish a detailed drawing indicating the required shaft dimensions, lift pit, overhead and machine room requirements to the customer in the general arrangement drawing. The customer will be solely responsible for keeping the lift shaft ready as per the drawing without involving the company in any manner.
    2. The Customer shall design and furnish a properly framed and enclosed lift shaft as per the drawing furnished by the company. If necessary, the customer shall provide extension of the lift shaft above the roof to withstand the forces and loads resulting from use of the lift. The customer shall arrange for keeping the lift shaft illuminated, treated and painted to minimize the accumulation and circulation of dust.
    3. The customer shall furnish a lift pit as per drawing furnished by the company below the lowest landing. The customer shall provide suitable drains and waterproofing. In addition, the customer shall also provide a steel ladder to access the pit.
    4. The customer shall furnish a properly illuminated and ventilated fireproof machine room to the company as per the drawing furnished by the company. The customer shall also furnish a proper stair case with handrails to access the machine room along with flame proof door opening to outside as per the fire safety norms.
    5. The customer shall get the machine room walls, floor and ceiling treated and painted to minimize accumulation and circulation of dust. The customer shall get the above works as narrated in points 1 to 5 before commencement of installation of the elevator.
    6. As the installation of the elevator by the company will have different stages, the customer shall furnish and install necessary lift shaft door frames, architraves, brick fascias/ steel fascia plates.
    7. The customer shall provide a continuous sill bearing area for lift shaft entrance of such constructions as to assure secure anchorage and support for each sill.
    8. The customer shall get all cutting of walls, floors or partitions together with any repairs done at appropriate stages of installation of elevator including grouting of all bolts, sills, members indicator and button boxes etc. in position.
