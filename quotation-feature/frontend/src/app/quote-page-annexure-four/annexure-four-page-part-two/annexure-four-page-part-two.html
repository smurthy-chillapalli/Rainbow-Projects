1. The customer shall provide scaffolding in the lift shaft as per the requirements of the erectors of the company during the erection period and to remove the same thereafter. If the height of the lift shaft is more than 40 meters, the customer shall provide steel scaffolding.

    3. The customer shall provide adequate safety and security measures to prevent any damage, theft or pilferage of material during storage, erection period and until the elevator is handed over to the customer. It is the sole responsibility of the customer to ensure the safety and protection of the materials in his custody.
    4. The customer shall provide hoisting beam or hoisting hooks in the machine room ceiling and rolled steel sections with bearing plates for support of the machine, if required.
    5. The customer shall provide living accommodation (complete with illumination, running water and sanitary facilities) for the installation staff of the company at or near to the site.
    6. The customer shall indemnify and keep the company harmless against all liability arising out of failure of the customer to carry out any of the foregoing.
