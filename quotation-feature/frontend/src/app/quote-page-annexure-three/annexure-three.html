Annexure III - Delivery, installation, Completion, Free Maintenance /warranty

  • DELIVERY AND ERECTION:
    1. We shall complete the supply, erection, testing and commissioning of elevator within 6-8 weeks from the date of handing over of the site complete in all respects including all civil works, shaft and machine room lighting and necessary staircase for the machine room and provision of scaffolding as per our requirements. Kindly note that our delivery and installation schedule will reckon from the date of handing over the lift shaft and also subject to providing the necessary civil and electrical works from your end as required at various stages of installation.
    2. The above completion period is subject to your handing over to us for uninterrupted use a clear dry elevator pit, shaft and machine room complete in all respects as per approved layout drawing along with preparatory work and power supply 8 weeks before the completion date indicated in above and subject to you providing continuous civil and electrical works there after at various stages of installation.
    3. If during the inspection of site, we observe that there is a delay in non completion of the elevator pit, shaft and machine room or non availability of power supply, we may at our option delay the transport of material to site so as to synchronize with the hoist way and machine room completion date. In such an event, a fresh completion date shall be determined depending upon minimum installation time indicated as above.
    4. The Elevators supplied is custom made depending upon your specified requirement. As such the same will not be taken back under any circumstances.
  • FREE MAINTENANCE AND GUARANTEE:
    1. Our quotation includes for 12 months free maintenance subject to you making full payment of the contract value upon handing over of elevator. The period of maintenances shall be deemed to be the date of handing over of elevator to customer.
    2. The materials supplied by us to your site shall have guarantee of 18 months from the date of our supply or 12 months from the date of installation whichever is earlier. In the event of the elevator is handled by third party for repairs without our consent in writing, the guarantee provided to elevator and its parts will cease to have guarantee. If customer does not make full payment of the contract value upon handing over of the elevator, the elevator and its parts shall have no guarantee.
